The process

Simple, fast, and done for you

We handle everything from ad design to USPS delivery.
You approve it, we execute. Most clients go from first call to live campaign in under three weeks.

01

Pick Your Market

Choose the Connecticut town you want to dominate. We target USPS routes in the highest-income zip codes so your ad lands in the best homes.

02

Claim Your Category

Lock in your industry slot before a competitor does. Only one roofer, one barber, one realtor per campaign. First come, first served.

03

We Build Your Ad

Our team writes your copy, designs your ad block, crafts your offer, and builds a custom QR tracking page. Zero work required from you.

04

We Mail and Track

We handle printing and USPS Every Door Direct Mail delivery. After the drop you receive a full QR scan and engagement report.
